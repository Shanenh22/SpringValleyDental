What to Expect at Your First Visit

We've designed your first appointment to be comprehensive yet comfortable, giving us a complete picture of your oral health.

👋

Warm Welcome & Check-in

5 minutes

Our team will greet you personally and verify your information. If you completed forms online, we'll simply confirm everything is current.

Bring with you: Photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications
🏥

Office Tour & Meet Your Team

10 minutes

We'll show you around our modern office and introduce you to the team members who will be caring for you.

You'll see: Treatment rooms, sterilization area, patient amenities, technology we use
🔍

Comprehensive Examination

30 minutes

Dr. Aletaha or Dr. Houshmand will perform a thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and oral tissues.

Includes: Digital X-rays, oral cancer screening, gum health assessment, bite analysis
✨

Professional Cleaning

45 minutes

Our skilled dentist will provide a gentle yet thorough cleaning and polish.

Service includes: Plaque removal, tartar removal, polishing, fluoride treatment
💬

Treatment Discussion

15 minutes

We'll discuss our findings and create a personalized treatment plan that fits your needs and budget.

We'll cover: Any treatment recommendations, timeline options, cost estimates, prevention tips

Your Comfort is Our Priority

We understand that dental visits can cause anxiety. That's why we offer multiple comfort options to ensure you feel relaxed and at ease.

😴 Sedation Dentistry

Nitrous Oxide (Laughing Gas)

Mild sedation that helps you relax while remaining fully conscious. Effects wear off quickly after treatment.

  • ✓ Safe for all ages
  • ✓ No recovery time needed
  • ✓ Can drive yourself home

Oral Conscious Sedation

Prescription medication taken before your appointment for deeper relaxation during longer procedures.

  • ✓ Minimal memory of procedure
  • ✓ Ideal for extensive treatments
  • ✓ Requires driver to/from appointment

🏨 Comfort Amenities

Entertainment Options

Netflix, music, ear plugs to help you relax during treatment.

Comfortable Environment

Heated blankets, ergonomic chairs, and neck pillows to create a spa-like experience.

Pain-Free Techniques

Topical anesthetics, gentle injection techniques, and the latest in pain management.

Take Breaks

Raise your hand anytime you need a break. We go at your pace, not ours.

Managing Dental Anxiety

Before Your Visit:

  • Arrive 15 minutes early to get comfortable
  • Bring your own music or audiobook
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Let us know about your concerns when scheduling

We're Here to Help:

  • Our team is trained in anxiety management techniques
  • We explain each step before we begin
  • Hand signals let you communicate during treatment
  • Take as many breaks as you need
Emergency Dental Care

During Office Hours

Call us immediately at (972) 852-2222

We reserve same-day appointments for dental emergencies and will see you as quickly as possible.

After Hours & Weekends

Call our main number and follow the prompts for emergency care instructions.

Dr. Aletaha and Dr. Houshmand provide on-call guidance for urgent situations.

What Constitutes an Emergency?

  • Severe tooth pain
  • Knocked out tooth
  • Broken or cracked teeth
  • Lost fillings or crowns
  • Abscess or swelling
  • Trauma to mouth or jaw
