How Invisalign works

You’ll wear a series of custom-made, removable aligners that apply gentle, consistent pressure to move teeth. Most patients switch to a new set every 1–2 weeks, wearing aligners 20–22 hours per day.

Digital planning

We map your tooth movement and preview your result before you start.

Removable trays

Take aligners out to eat, drink anything besides water, and brush or floss.

Smart features

Many plans use small tooth‑colored “attachments” that help guide precise movement.

Treatment timeline

Exact timing depends on your goals and case complexity. Many adult cases finish in about 12–18 months with consistent wear; simpler cases can be shorter.

Wear time

20–22 hours daily; change trays as directed.

Visits

Periodic check‑ins to confirm tracking and comfort.

Retention

After treatment, retainers keep your new smile stable.

Care & maintenance

  • Rinse aligners and brush them gently each time you brush your teeth.
  • Avoid hot water and colored mouthwashes that can warp or stain trays.
  • Store aligners in their case when not wearing them.

Who’s a good candidate?

Clear aligners can address many mild to moderate spacing, crowding, and bite concerns. Some complex cases—especially those requiring extractions or jaw correction—may be better served with other orthodontic approaches. We’ll advise you at your consultation.

Why Dallas Patients Love Invisalign

Invisalign uses a series of custom‑made, clear aligner trays to gently move teeth into their ideal position. Clear aligner therapy offers several advantages: the braces are virtually invisible, comfortable to wear and removable for eating and brushing. The aligners apply gentle pressure to guide teeth into place and use small tooth‑colored attachments to enhance or assist specific movements.

Because the trays are nearly invisible and removable, you can straighten your teeth without the look or dietary restrictions of traditional braces. However, aligners must be worn consistently — about 20–22 hours per day — to be effective. Clear aligners are best suited for mild to moderate spacing and crowding; complex cases or severe bite issues may still require braces.

How Invisalign Works – Step by Step

First, we capture digital impressions and create a personalized treatment plan. You’ll wear each set of aligners for about two weeks, removing them only to eat, drink, brush and floss. Regular check‑ups ensure your progress is on track until you achieve your new smile. One of the main challenges of clear aligner therapy is compliance; if aligners are not worn as prescribed, treatment can take longer or may not be successful.

Invisalign vs. Traditional Braces

Invisalign aligners are smooth and removable, making them a popular choice for adults and teens seeking a discreet alternative to metal brackets. Traditional braces remain the best option for severe crowding, large gaps or complex jaw discrepancies. During your consultation, we’ll evaluate your bite and recommend the most effective approach.
