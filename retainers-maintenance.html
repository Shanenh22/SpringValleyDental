Orthodontic Retainers & Maintenance

Protect Your Orthodontic Investment

After completing orthodontic treatment, retainers are essential to maintain your beautiful new smile. Without proper retention, teeth can shift back toward their original positions, undoing years of orthodontic work. Our custom retainers ensure your smile stays perfect for life.

Why Retainers Are Essential

After orthodontic treatment, your teeth, gums, and bones need time to stabilize in their new positions. During this critical period, retainers hold your teeth in place while the surrounding tissues adapt and strengthen.

What Happens Without Retainers:

  • Orthodontic Relapse: Teeth gradually shift back toward their original positions
  • Crowding Returns: Front teeth often become crowded again
  • Bite Changes: Your bite may shift, affecting jaw function
  • Investment Lost: Years of orthodontic treatment become undone
  • Additional Costs: May require retreatment with braces or aligners

Studies show that up to 50% of people experience some degree of orthodontic relapse without proper retainer use. The good news is that consistent retainer wear virtually eliminates this risk.

Types of Retainers

We offer several types of retainers to fit your lifestyle and maintenance preferences. Your orthodontist will recommend the best option based on your specific needs.

Clear Plastic Retainers (Essix)

$100 - $300 per set
Lasts 1-3 years

Custom-molded clear plastic retainers that fit snugly over your teeth. Nearly invisible and comfortable to wear.

Pros

  • Nearly invisible
  • Easy to clean
  • Comfortable fit
  • Affordable to replace

Cons

  • Can crack or break
  • May stain over time
  • Need replacement every 1-3 years
  • Must remove for eating

Hawley Retainers (Wire & Acrylic)

$150 - $350 per retainer
Lasts 5-10 years

Traditional retainers with a plastic base and wire framework. Highly durable and adjustable by your orthodontist.

Pros

  • Very durable
  • Adjustable if needed
  • Long lifespan
  • Can be personalized

Cons

  • More visible than clear
  • Speech adjustment period
  • Bulkier design
  • Higher upfront cost

Permanent Retainers (Fixed/Bonded)

$200 - $600 per arch
Lasts 10-20 years

Thin wire bonded to the back of your front teeth. Provides 24/7 retention without removable appliances.

Pros

  • No compliance needed
  • Invisible from front
  • Long-term solution
  • Excellent retention

Cons

  • Harder to clean
  • Can break or debond
  • Requires special flossing

Daily Care & Cleaning

Do:

  • Rinse retainers with cool water after removal
  • Brush gently with a soft toothbrush (no abrasive toothpaste)
  • Soak weekly in a retainer/aligner cleaner as directed
  • Store in a ventilated case when not in use

Don’t:

  • Use hot water (can warp plastic)
  • Use bleach, alcohol, or harsh chemicals
  • Wrap in napkins (easy to throw away by accident)
  • Leave within reach of pets

Wearing Schedule & Replacements

First 6 Months

Full-time wear (22 hours/day) unless instructed otherwise. Remove only for eating and cleaning.

6–12 Months

Transition to nighttime-only wear as advised. Continue excellent oral hygiene and regular checkups.

Beyond 12 Months

Nighttime wear long-term to prevent relapse. Replace retainers if cracked, warped, loose, or stained.

Tip: Consider ordering a backup set of clear retainers to avoid lapse in wear if one is lost or damaged.

When to Call Us

Contact our office if your retainer cracks, loses fit, rubs or causes sores, or if you notice your teeth shifting. We can adjust, repair, or replace your retainer to keep your smile stable.
